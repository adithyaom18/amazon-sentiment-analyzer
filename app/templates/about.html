Natural Language Processing Pipeline
  1. Text Cleaning: Remove URLs, special characters, and convert to lowercase
  2. Tokenization: Split text into individual words/tokens
  3. Stopword Removal: Remove common words that don't carry meaning
  4. Lemmatization: Reduce words to their base/dictionary form
  5. Feature Extraction: Convert text to TF-IDF numerical features
  6. Classification: Predict sentiment using trained machine learning model
Dataset

The model was trained on the Amazon Fine Food Reviews dataset from Kaggle, containing over 500,000 food reviews from Amazon.

For development and testing, we used a balanced sample of 1,000 reviews (500 positive, 500 negative).
